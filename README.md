# Superstore Sales Dashboard (Tableau)
## Overivew 
This project features an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Superstore sales data across the United States. It provides key insights into revenue performance, product trends, and geographic distribution at the city level.
The dashboard includes KPI tracking, time-series analysis, and top product identification, enabling clear visualization of sales patterns and key business drivers.

## Key Features
- KPI metrics: Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value  
- Monthly sales trend analysis to identify growth patterns  
- Geographic visualization of sales distribution across the U.S.  
- Top 10 products by revenue to highlight key business drivers

## Tools & Techniques 
- Tableau Dashboard (data visualization & dashboard design)
- Data analysis and aggregation  
- Time-series analysis

## Key Insights
- Sales demonstrate a steady upward trend over time, indicating sustained business growth.
- Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of top-performing products, revealing key drivers of sales performance.
- Geographic analysis shows variation in sales across regions and cities, highlighting potential areas for targeted business expansion.
